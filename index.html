About OVD-info

OVD-info is an independent human rights media project.

Our mission is to collect, provide and analyze data on political repressions in Russia.

Since December 2011 we monitors politically motivated arrests during peaceful assemblies in Russia.

And provide this data to media, human rights organisations and research institutions.

Our website: http://ovdinfo.org
Follow us: @ovdinfo

Arrests & Events

We publish data in real time (http://ovdinfo.org/express-news)

User can organize news by subjects, dates, regions filters

And by police station, law articles, events and some other tags

All news are linked by events

The process of news creating is standardized

The full story on detention will provide us data both on Event and Arrested people

Event:
Date, region, Place, agreement, Type, Subject, Organizer, Description

Arrests:
Names, Number, Police station(s), Court verdict (optional)
